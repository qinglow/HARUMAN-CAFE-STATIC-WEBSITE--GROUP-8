OUR HISTORY

Haruman Cafe intertwines flavor and memory, transfrming eah sip into a treasured moment and fostering connections among all who come through our doors.

Haruman Cafe

Haruman (하루만) is a Korean word that translates to "Just one day" it was inspired by BTS' song which compels us to reflect on "valuing each day." A 2014 study found a direct link between the region of the brain responsible for taste memory and the area responsible for encoding the time and place we experienced the taste. Haruman cafe is precisely just that a place to create memories, no matter whether it is big or small. No matter which part of the day. Whether you' re just starting the day or ending the day with your loved ones. One sip is a memory that unfolds, one we treasure, and one we will keep coming back to.
